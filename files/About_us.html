About Us

Sharebuddy is a project made by Santosh Sahoo, Ajitesh Madhu, Agam Arora, Prashant Singh & Aryan Chutani. The project provides solutions to allow as many people as possible to share their recurring expenses in a group, mainly related to services including video and audio streaming, online games, software licenses or digital newspapers. Most of them can be shared, with grouped packages designed by the publishers.

The miracle is this:
The more we share, more we have !
